Our Programmes

Undergraduate Programme

The university offers a variety of undergraduate programs with unique curricula tailored to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of their chosen field.
Admission requirements typically include a high school diploma or equivalent, standardized test scores, and an application essay. Graduates from our programs have successful careers in business, engineering, healthcare, and the arts.
The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major and Major-Minor as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
Learn more

Graduates Programme

our university provides a wide range of graduate programs, such as Master's and Ph.D. degrees. These programs offer advanced knowledge and specialized training in specific areas of study.
Admission requirements typically include a bachelor's degree in a related field, letters of recommendation, a statement of purpose, and sometimes standardized test scores.
Our graduate programs cover various research areas, enabling students to explore their chosen field in depth and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their respective disciplines.
Learn more

Short Courses

The university provides flexible short courses for skill enhancement or specialized knowledge without the commitment of a full degree. These accessible programs cover diverse topics, from professional development to personal enrichment. Minimal admission requirements make them an excellent avenue for acquiring new skills or exploring new interests.
Our eight week intensive certificate course is open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, school graduates, workers and the general public.
