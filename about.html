About

Welcome to the Computer Science Department of University of Ghana.
Our department has been a leader in computer science education and research over years since its establishment in 1954.

Our faculty is composed of highly qualified professors and researchers who are passionate about advancing the field of computer science. They specialize in various areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering. Their dedication ensures that students receive an excellent education and have access to cutting-edge research opportunities.

At University of Ghana, we believe in hands-on learning and provide state-of-the-art facilities to support our students' academic and research pursuits. Our department is equipped with modern laboratories, including specialized spaces for robotics, virtual reality, and data analysis. These facilities allow students to gain practical experience and apply their theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios.

We are proud of our department's contributions to the computer science community. Our faculty and students have received recognition for their research achievements and have published numerous papers in prestigious conferences and journals. We also actively collaborate with industry partners to ensure our curriculum remains relevant and up-to-date with the latest industry trends.
