Student Resource

Welcome to the Student Resources center of the Computer Science Department! Our primary goal is to provide comprehensive support to our students throughout their academic path. Take advantage of the diverse range of resources available to enrich your learning experience and achieve academic success.

Academic Advice

Our experienced academic advisors are here to assist you in navigating your program. Whether you have inquiries about course selection, career options, or academic planning, our advisors are prepared to offer guidance. Schedule a meeting to ensure you are on the right track for success.

Internship Opportunities

Acquire practical experience through our internship programs. Discover chances to apply your classroom knowledge in real-world settings by collaborating with industry professionals. Our department partners with leading companies to offer valuable internships that can shape your future career.

Scholarship Information

Explore scholarship opportunities to support your education. We offer a range of scholarships based on academic achievement, research excellence, and contributions to the computer science community. Check eligibility criteria and application deadlines to make the most of available financial support.

Career Services

Prepare for your future career with the help of our career services. Access resume-building workshops, interview preparation, and job placement assistance. Our goal is to empower you with the skills and resources needed to excel in the competitive field of computer science.

Student Organizations

Engage with like-minded peers through our student organizations. Join clubs and societies related to computer science to network, collaborate on projects, and stay updated on industry trends. Connect with fellow students who share your passion for technology.

Tutoring Services

Need additional support with coursework? Take advantage of our tutoring services. Our department offers peer tutoring and academic resources to ensure you have the assistance you need to succeed academically.

Student Success Workshops

Attend workshops designed to enhance your academic and professional skills. From time management to coding bootcamps, these workshops are tailored to help you thrive in your studies and beyond.
